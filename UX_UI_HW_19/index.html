Taylor Knauff

One of the most exhilarating aspects of UX/UI design for me is transforming a client's vision into a sleek, user-friendly product that captivates and retains users. My design philosophy revolves around striking the perfect balance between user-centric functionality and cutting-edge aesthetics. Hailing from vibrant Denver, CO, I bring six months of dedicated experience in product design to the table. Despite being relatively new to the field, my passion and dedication make me a force to be reckoned with.

My UX Skills

User Research

I harness user research to fuel innovative UX/UI designs. With a toolkit of interviews, surveys, and usability tests, I uncover deep insights into user needs and behaviors. My findings translate into actionable recommendations, personas, and journey maps that drive user-centered design and elevate the overall user experience.

User Interface Design

I design striking and intuitive interfaces that captivate users and achieve business objectives. Merging visual flair with practical functionality, I create seamless, engaging experiences through meticulous attention to layout, interactive elements, and responsive design. My work transforms ideas into visually stunning and user-friendly interfaces that not only look great but also drive meaningful interactions
